About PayVega

What is PayVega?
Payvega is bank account for the current generation. It is a mobile first bank made keeping in mind the current and future needs of the people who demand more from their money.
Who can sign up for Payvega?
Any Indian citizen, above the age of 18, with a valid PAN card and Aadhar card can sign up for Payvega account.
Is Payvega a bank?
Yes and No. Payvega lets people open an account and issues a debit card just like a bank. But Payvega is not a bank as they don't store the money. The money is stored with our partner bank Yes Bank.
What is the maximum amount I can store in the account?
Currently the maximum balance that you store in your account is Rs. 50,000
I want to issue and receive cheques?
Payvega is made keeping the future in mind. Cheques as payment instrument are dying and electronic payments making up for the bulk of all payments. Hence Payvega does not support both receipt and issuance of cheques.
What is the rate of interest offered in this account?
RBI currently does not allow us to pay interest in this account. But watch this space we may have something to announce soon :)

About Debit Card

Which card do I get?
All people who sign up get a Payvega, Visa branded debit card.
Where can I use the card?
The Payvega card can be used at all merchant terminals (Point of Sale) which accept Visa. It can also be used online at all merchants who accept Visa Cards.
Is this card accepted internationally?
No this card cannot be used internationally.
Can I withdraw cash at an ATM?
Yes Payvega cards can be used at any ATM across the country to withdraw cash.

Quick Account Opening

How can I open an account?
Payvega is currently by invitation only. Sign up for the invitation using your email id. We will email you the link to sign up within some time.
What documents will I need to open an account?
We don't need any document to open an account. You must however be holding a valid PAN card and Aadhar card.
How do I deposit money in the account?
Currently you can deposit money via NEFT into the account. Soon however will be accepting Net Banking, IMPS, Cash and many more. The best way would be to tell your employer to deposit your salary into the account via NEFT for free.

About Money Transfer

How can I transfer money in and out of the account?
Money can be transferred into the account currently via NEFT. Money can be transferred out using NEFT. Money can also be withdrawn from ATM's and the card is useable both online and offline for shopping.
Can I send money via Facebook or email?
No, but we are working on making this easily possible and will be available soon.
What is the cap on the transfers?
There are no caps on transfers. All the money can be transferred in and out any number of times within a day, month or year.

About Saving Goals

What are saving goals?
Savings goals are like accounts within an account. You can set up multiple Saving Goals. You can leave standing instructions and rules for whatever you want.
How does saving goals work?
Once you set up a goal you can transfer any amount of money into the goal. Once the goal is met you can delete the goal and all money will be available. You can set it up so that money keeps depositing into the goal at regular intervals or upon rules set by you.
How many goals can be setup?
As many as you like. We do not set up any limits.
Does the money in the goal add to total balance?
Yes it does. And the total balance cannot exceed 50,000 at any point.
Can I use the money in the goal by mistake?
The money set aside in the goal will not be useable while transferring or at ATM's or even at any merchants. To use it you will have to delete the goal.

About Security

How secure is the money?
Very secure. The money is sitting safely with Yes Bank Ltd, one of the best banks there is. The money stored in your account is guaranteed by both Yes Bank and the RBI.
How secure is the app?
We deploy the most sophisticated security software to prevent anything happening to your account. We have a security team monitoring the data 24 x 7. To further minimize risk no data is stored either with us or on your phone. It is safely stored with the bank on their servers which have not been breached EVER.

Invite

There are a lot of people who have signed up. We send out invites periodically in first come first served order. And no we do not hand out invites because "Tu janta nahi mera baap koun hai" do not scare us.
